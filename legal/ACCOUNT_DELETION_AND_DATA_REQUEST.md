# BiMakas Account Deletion and Data Request Policy

## English

Effective Date: March 1, 2026  
Last Updated: March 1, 2026

This document explains how user account deletion, data deletion, and data access requests are handled.

## 1. Account Deletion Request

Users can submit an account deletion request:

- Through the profile/settings area in the app (if available), or
- By emailing info@bimakas.com using their registered email address.

## 2. Effects After Deletion

After the account deletion process:

- Access to the account is terminated.
- Profile data and related content are deleted or anonymized, except where legal or technical obligations require retention.
- Records required for ongoing legal/accounting obligations may be retained for the required period.

## 3. Data Access / Correction / Deletion Requests

Users may submit requests for:

- Learning what data is stored
- Correcting inaccurate data
- Deleting specific data
- Objecting to processing activities

Request email: info@bimakas.com

## 4. Verification

For security reasons, account ownership may be verified before a request is processed.

## 5. Processing Timelines

Requests are finalized within a reasonable time, considering legal timelines and operational workload.

## 1. Hesap Silme Talebi

Kullanici, hesap silme talebini:

- Uygulama icindeki profil/ayarlar alanindan (mevcutsa), veya
- info@bimakas.com adresine kayitli e-postasi ile ulasarak

iletebilir.

## 2. Silme Sonrasi Etkiler

Hesap silme islemi sonrasinda:

- Hesaba erisim sonlandirilir.
- Profil verileri ve iliskili icerikler, mevzuat ve teknik zorunluluklar disinda silinir veya anonimlestirilir.
- Devam eden yasal/muhasebesel yukumluluklar kapsamindaki kayitlar gerekli sure boyunca saklanabilir.

## 3. Veri Erisim / Duzeltme / Silme Talepleri

Kullanici asagidaki talepleri iletebilir:

- Hangi verilerin tutuldugunu ogrenme
- Yanlis verilerin duzeltilmesi
- Belirli verilerin silinmesi
- Isleme faaliyetlerine itiraz

Talepler icin e-posta: info@bimakas.com

## 4. Dogrulama

Guvenlik nedeniyle, talep isleme alinmadan once hesap sahipligi dogrulanabilir.

## 5. Sonuclanma Sureleri

Talepler, mevzuattaki sureler ve operasyonel yogunluk dikkate alinarak makul surede sonuclandirilir.
