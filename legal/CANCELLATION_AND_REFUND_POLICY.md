# BiMakas Cancellation and Refund Policy

## English

Effective Date: March 1, 2026  
Last Updated: March 1, 2026

## 1. Scope

This policy defines cancellation, refund, and reimbursement processes for appointments created through BiMakas.

## 2. User-Initiated Cancellation

- Users can submit a cancellation request through the app before the appointment time.
- The refund rate may vary depending on the remaining time before the appointment.
- For cancellations made less than 24 hours before the appointment, refunds may be partially or fully denied.

## 3. Provider-Initiated Cancellation

In cancellations caused by the provider, if payment has been collected, the refund process is initiated with priority.

## 4. Technical or System-Related Cancellations

For transactions that cannot be completed due to technical reasons, the case is reviewed; refunds are applied when required.

## 5. Refund Method and Timeline

- Refunds are processed using the same method as the original payment.
- Approved refunds may take approximately 3-10 business days to appear on the card/bank account, depending on the bank.

## 6. Cash Payment Scenarios

For appointments with cash payment options, depending on the nature of the refund flow, field collection/cancellation records may be used instead of in-app refunds.

## 7. Objections and Support

To object to a refund/cancellation decision or request a status check:

- Email: info@bimakas.com
- Web: https://bimakas.com/

## Turkce

## 1. Kapsam

Bu politika, BiMakas uzerinden olusturulan randevularin iptal, iade ve odeme iade sureclerini tanimlar.

## 2. Kullanici Tarafli Iptal

- Kullanici, randevu saatinden once uygulama uzerinden iptal talebi olusturabilir.
- Iade orani, randevuya kalan sureye gore degisebilir.
- Randevu saatine 24 saatten az kala yapilan iptallerde iade kismen veya tamamen reddedilebilir.

## 3. Berber/Saglayici Kaynakli Iptal

Saglayici kaynakli iptal durumunda, odeme alinmis ise iade sureci oncelikli baslatilir.

## 4. Teknik veya Sistemsel Iptaller

Teknik nedenlerle tamamlanamayan islemlerde durum incelemesi yapilir; gerekli ise iade uygulanir.

## 5. Iade Yontemi ve Suresi

- Iadeler, odemenin yapildigi yonteme uygun sekilde gerceklestirilir.
- Onaylanan iadelerin karta/banka hesabina yansimasi bankaya gore degiserek ortalama 3-10 is gunu surebilir.

## 6. Nakit Odeme Senaryolari

Nakit odeme secenekli randevularda iade akisinin niteligine gore uygulama ici iade yerine sahada tahsilat/iptal kayitlari esas alinabilir.

## 7. Itiraz ve Destek

Iade/iptal kararina itiraz etmek veya durum sorgulamak icin:

- E-posta: info@bimakas.com
- Web: https://bimakas.com/
